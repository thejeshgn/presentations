What's OpenCity?

OpenCity.in is an Urban Data Portal

A collaboration between Oorvani Foundation and DataMeet.

It's a minimum viable product to prove the idea

Idea

Opening up data beyond academic use

Make it simple and searchable*

Documents are also data

Just enough technology to deliver the service

Build Community

We already have our researchers on the DataMeet list and github. They can use the data from there.

Implementation

A simple website

Data - CSV, PDF or KMLs

No API's

Optimize it for Google and other search engines

Getting Things Done and Get Back to Work

Choose popular formats that's used by the user and not by us. Also keep it to a limited amount.

How did it go?

We have 40 hrs per week. We had to clean the data and then upload it takes time. You can see the repos of OpenCity, Open Bangalore and DataMeet for the code used for scraping and cleaning.
