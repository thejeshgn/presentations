Why Real Time Polls?

Lets see some examples

Media house

Budget session is on and you want to know the reaction of people.

Political Entity

You want to talk to your supporters for exit poll.

Not for Profit

You want to talk to every school to find out if the mid meals was hot or not.

Activism

You want people to call their representatives by just entering their phone number and pincode.

But our method needs to be?

  • Scalable - Reach thousands in matter of minutes
  • Realtime - I should be able to see as we get info
  • Accuracy - Clean Data
  • Target - Reach our audience

So we need

We need a computer to call all our contacts simultaneously and conduct poll. We also want it to update database live so we can control and play with it.
